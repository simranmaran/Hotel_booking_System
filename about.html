About Royal Stay

A Tradition of Excellence

Established in 1985, Royal Stay has been providing exceptional hospitality services for over three decades. What started as a boutique hotel with just 15 rooms has now grown into a luxurious establishment with over 200 rooms across multiple locations.

Our vision is to create unforgettable experiences for every guest who walks through our doors. We believe in personalizing service to meet the unique needs of each individual, ensuring that your stay with us is nothing short of perfect.

From the architecture of our buildings to the design of our rooms, every element of Royal Stay has been carefully curated to provide an atmosphere of luxury and comfort. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to maintain the highest standards of service and to ensure that your experience exceeds expectations.
